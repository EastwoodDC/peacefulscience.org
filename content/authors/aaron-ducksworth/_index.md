Aaron Ducksworth is a doctoral student at Southeastern Seminary. He holds a B.S. in Interdisciplinary Studies from Mississippi State University, a M.Div., and a ThM in Theology from Southeastern. He is currently pursuing a PhD in Christian Ethics. Aaron researches at the interface of Contextual Theology and Social Ethics and explores the ways socio-cultural factors shape theological formation and ethical engagement.
